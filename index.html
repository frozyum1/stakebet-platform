FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do I need a gaming licence to launch?

Yes — you operate the casino under your own gaming licence. Ludocore is the software and infrastructure provider; you are the operator of record. We can guide you to the right licensing jurisdiction as part of onboarding. Curaçao and Anjouan are common starting points for crypto-native operators and can be obtained in parallel with your technical setup.

How long does it take to go live?

Typically 4–6 weeks from contract signing to live production. This covers branding and configuration, payment account integration, KYC provider connection, QA with your team and the final production deployment. Licensing timelines run in parallel and are independent of the technical launch.

What exactly is included in the $40,000 setup fee?

Everything needed to get a production casino live under your brand: branding configuration (logo, colours, domain), full platform deployment on managed Kubernetes, game provider integration (2,600+ games), payment account wiring, KYC provider connection, admin backoffice setup and a full QA and handover session with your team.

What games are included?

2,600+ games from multiple providers including Pragmatic Play, Evolution, Hacksaw, Spribe, BGaming and Evoplay — covering slots, live casino, table games and crash titles. Additionally, 8 in-house provably-fair Originals (Crash, Dice, Mines, Plinko, Coinflip, Limbo, Keno, Pool) are included with zero content fee — they run on your platform's house edge with no third-party revenue share.

What does the backoffice let me manage?

Everything: user search and management, KYC document review and approval, withdrawal approvals, manual wallet adjustments, game and provider enable/disable, financial reporting (GGR/NGR/ledger), real-time live dashboard, per-player event audit trail, bonus and promotion management, affiliate tracking, leaderboard and tournament management, and granular admin role permissions.

How is the monthly fee calculated?

$5,000 per month or 15% of your net gaming revenue (NGR = GGR minus bonuses and chargebacks), whichever is greater. At low-volume months you pay the platform minimum; as your casino scales, the fee scales with it. Final terms are agreed per partner.

Can I fully brand it as my own casino?

Absolutely. Your logo, domain, colour scheme and player-facing identity. Players never see any Ludocore branding. The player app and backoffice are configured to your brand during onboarding.

What cryptocurrencies are supported?

Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Tether (USDT), Litecoin (LTC) and additional currencies configurable at launch. The platform is built crypto-native — instant on-chain deposits and withdrawals, multi-currency wallets, with a double-entry ledger and full reconciliation.

Is hosting and infrastructure included?

Yes. Managed Kubernetes, PostgreSQL, Redis, monitoring (Prometheus + Grafana), daily automated backups with 30-day retention, Cloudflare DDoS protection and CI/CD pipelines are all included in the monthly platform fee. You do not manage any infrastructure.

What ongoing support is included?

Dedicated technical support, continuous security and dependency updates, new feature releases and infrastructure maintenance. Support SLA details are agreed per partner.
