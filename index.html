FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do I need a gaming licence?

Yes. You operate the casino under your own gaming licence — Ludocore provides the software. We guide you to the right jurisdiction (e.g. Curaçao or Anjouan) as part of onboarding. This keeps the model clean: you're the operator, we're the software provider.

How long does it take to launch?

Typically a few weeks once your licence and payment accounts are in place. Branding, integration, testing and go-live run in parallel with your licensing, so neither track blocks the other.

What games are included?

2,600+ games — 1,700+ slots, live casino, table games, and in-house provably-fair Originals — from providers such as Pragmatic Play, Evolution, Hacksaw, Spribe, BGaming and Evoplay.

How much does it cost?

A one-time setup fee plus a monthly platform fee or a software revenue-share, whichever is greater. Anchor-partner (founder) pricing is available for early partners. Final commercial terms are agreed per partner.

Is it crypto-native?

Yes. The platform is built for crypto — instant on-chain deposits and withdrawals, multi-currency, with a double-entry ledger and reconciliation under the hood. Fiat rails can be added via your payment partners.

Can I fully brand it as my own?

Absolutely. Your logo, colours, domain and identity — it's your casino, not a Ludocore-branded skin.
